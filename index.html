About Albania

Albania is a country in Southeast Europe, located on the Balkan Peninsula. With its stunning landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture, Albania is a hidden gem for travelers.

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Tirana
  • Official language: Albanian
  • Population: Over 2.8 million
  • Famous for: Its beaches, ancient ruins, and hospitality
